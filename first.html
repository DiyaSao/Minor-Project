Courses We Offer

Here are some courses that we offer the students for their career.

INTERMEDIATE

The Intermediate Courses are taught at the Government or Private or Cooperative Junior Colleges for two years. These Courses are followed by prestigious State level Entrance Tests for admission to the various Degree Colleges offering Professional or general education.

DEGREE

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.), Bachelor of Arts (B.A.), and Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) to specialized programs like Bachelor of Performing Arts or Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), the degree courses list is extensive which are generally 3 years degree courses after 12th.

POST GRADUATION

Post-graduation is an advanced academic program that are pursued following the completion of an undergraduate degree. The courses offer specific knowledge and skills in a specific field.
